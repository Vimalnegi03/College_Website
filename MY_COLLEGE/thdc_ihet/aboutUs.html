THDC Institute of Hydropower Engineering and Technology

The campus of institute is spread across a mighty twenty acres green land and known for its rich infrastructure. What makes this institute different from others is its lush green campus and pollution free environment. The campus is beautiful and ideally suited for the students interested in technical education the campus of THDC-IHET not only provides a tranquil place but abundance of greenery makes it an ideal place for studies as well. Moreover, the institute is situated near Tehri Lake which has become the most beautiful sight seen and a water adventure sports hub for travelers.

The institute provides grand Infrastructure, well equipped labs, modernized classrooms, computer centre, seminar hall and a fully automated library. In addition best possible facility of hostel, sports, gym, canteen and transportation are offered. Well qualified faculties of the institute provide best educational, research and professional environment.

Dr. Sharad K. Pradhan is working as Director, at the THDC-Institute of Hydropower Engineering and Technology, Tehri. Earlier he was a Professor, Mechanical Engineering, Dean, Corporate & International Relations, and Associate Director, of NITTTR Bhopal-Siemens Center of Excellence at the National Institute of Technical Teachers’ Training and Research (NITTTR), Bhopal. He did his Ph.D. from the Mechanical Engineering Department, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Delhi and before joining NITTTR Bhopal he was a Scientist in ‘Design & Simulation Group’ at ‘Central Mechanical Engineering Research Institute’ (CMERI), Durgapur, (WB).
